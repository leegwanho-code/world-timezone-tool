Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Overview

WorldClock ("we", "our", or "the Service") is a free, browser-based world time zone comparison tool available at worldtimezone.tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the Service and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

We do not require any registration or login. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

Automatically stored data (your browser only)

To remember your preferred cities and display theme between visits, the Service stores small pieces of data in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

  • wtt-cities — the list of cities you have added
  • wtt-theme — your light/dark mode preference

You can clear this data at any time via your browser's storage settings.

Server logs

Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) for security and operational purposes. These logs are governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

The Service displays advertisements served by Google AdSense (Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA). Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to show ads that may be relevant to your interests.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

4. Cookies

The Service itself does not set cookies. However, Google AdSense sets its own cookies to serve and measure advertisements. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements but will not affect the core functionality of the time zone comparison tool.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — hosting and content delivery. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Privacy Policy

All time zone calculations are performed entirely within your browser using the built-in Intl API. No data is sent to external APIs.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
